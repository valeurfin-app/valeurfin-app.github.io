📜 Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026 | v3.5

1. Data Collection

ValeurFin collects ZERO data on any external server. All information is stored exclusively on your device.

2. Storage

AES-256 encryption + IndexedDB. No cookies, no tracking, no third-party sharing.

3. Third-Party

MFAPI for fund search, Yahoo Finance for live prices (only stock symbols sent — no personal data), TradingView widgets for market data.

4. Deletion

Security → Wipe All Data to permanently delete.
